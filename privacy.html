Privacy Policy

At LEAD, we value and respect the privacy of our dashboard visitors, partners, customers and their end-users, and we believe that, in order for them to make informed choices about the use of their personal data, we must be transparent with respect to our privacy practices.

To ensure such transparency we have established the following Privacy Policy.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

When using or interacting with our dashboard, we may collect or receive the following types of information (collectively, “Information”).

  1. Personal Information such as name, email, contact details or any other personal content that you provide to us whether through a form or field on our app or any other communication (e.g. email, phone number).

  2. Technical Information such as device type, operating system, IP address and other similar technical information typically received from the device automatically when visiting or interacting with our Platforms. This may include the referring URL that led you to our app.

  3. Usage Information such as the pages you visited on our app, where you clicked, searches performed on our app and other similar information related to how you have used our app. It may also include information related to whether you receive, opened or clicked on any links in an email sent to you.
